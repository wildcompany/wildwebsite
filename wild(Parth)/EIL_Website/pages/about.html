About Emotional Intelligence in Leadership (EIL)

Understanding ourselves and others to lead with empathy, awareness, and purpose.

Our Story

How Personal Growth Became a Mission to Help Others

This journey grew out of my own real-life experience. While I was working on my skincare business project, I ended up diving into emotional intelligence and leadership studies.

It began with a simple motivation: I realized that knowing accounting, business registration, the science of skincare, and laws and regulations wasn’t enough if I couldn’t handle the human part of leadership. The emotional and cultural challenges, the misunderstandings, and the daily human interactions in my team were just as important as the technical work. Specifically, I wanted to improve how I related to one of my business team members who was also my friend at that time.

Consequently, during my supervised project in my Master’s program in International Business, I spent over 2,000 hours researching this subject. The result was a big change in me. I became calmer and more in control of my emotions, and people around me even commented on it. They told me I seemed much more at peace. At the same time, the friendship and work relationship with my friend and business team member improved significantly.

Later, I wrote a full pedagogical case study about this transformation in my program. This made me clearly see that it wasn’t just theory — it was a real, practical transformation that I had lived personally and inside my company.

Because of that experience, I decided to share these results and coach other leaders to lead with emotional intelligence, calm, and balance — and to bring a healthier emotional culture into their work and life.

Our Vision

We envision a community where emotional intelligence is understood as a practical, everyday skill — a kind of wisdom that helps people lead their emotions instead of being led by them. A community where leaders make decisions with calm, clarity, and understanding, and where this creates healthier interactions in workplaces, friendships, and the broader society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help leaders understand their emotions deeply, take ownership of them, and guide them wisely. Through practical learning and real-life application, we support leaders in becoming calmer, clearer, and more emotionally aware — so their decisions and interactions create thoughtful and balanced environments around them.

Our Core Values

Empathy and Human-Centered Wisdom

We encourage leaders to trust their inner wisdom and lead with empathy. Our goal is to help people communicate thoughtfully and create a more understanding, compassionate workplace.

Evidence-Based Empowerment

We use research-backed methods to empower individuals, giving them the confidence to grow and lead effectively. Our approach ensures that every strategy is grounded in evidence and real-world success.

Sustainable Work-Life Balance

We believe in a balanced approach to leadership development. Our programs are designed to prevent overwhelm and promote well-being, helping leaders and teams thrive without sacrificing their peace of mind.
